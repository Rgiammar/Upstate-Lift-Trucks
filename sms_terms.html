1. SMS Consent

By submitting a request form on upstatelifttrucks.com and providing your phone number, you expressly consent to receive SMS/MMS from Upstate Lift Trucks and up to two participating independent local service providers. This consent is not a condition of any purchase or service.

2. Types of Messages

  • Confirmation that your equipment request was received
  • Follow-up from Upstate Lift Trucks regarding your request
  • Contact from matched independent local service providers
  • Quotes, availability updates, or scheduling information

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 5 messages per inquiry combined from Upstate Lift Trucks and participating providers. You will not be enrolled in any recurring marketing SMS program without separate explicit consent.

4. How to Opt Out

  • Reply STOP to any text message
  • Email hello@upstatelifttrucks.com — subject: "SMS Opt Out"
  • Call (864) 214-6269

After opting out you will receive one confirmation message and no further texts from Upstate Lift Trucks.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Supported carriers include AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, Boost Mobile, U.S. Cellular, and MetroPCS.

8. Third-Party Provider Texting

When your request is matched with a provider, that provider may contact you directly via their own systems. Upstate Lift Trucks is not responsible for their communications. Opt out from a specific provider by contacting them directly.

9. TCPA Compliance

Upstate Lift Trucks complies with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA). We obtain express written consent before sending automated messages. Consent is documented at form submission including date, time, and consent language presented.

10. No Purchase Required

SMS consent is never required as a condition of our lead matching service, a quote, or any purchase from a participating provider.

Your phone number will not be sold to third-party marketers or data brokers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
